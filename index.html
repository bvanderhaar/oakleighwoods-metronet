Oakleigh Woods and Metronet FAQs

What is Metronet?

Metronet is an ISP (Internet Service Provider) similar to Comcast and AT&T. Metronet announced fiber build out on their website: Metronet Press Release

What will homeowners be required to do?

In short, nothing. Quoting the article: "Residents will receive communication by mail about construction activity in their neighborhood 30 days prior to starting. Additional messaging, such as yard signs, will also be provided to notify residents when construction is beginning in their neighborhood."Source

What is fiber to the home (FTTH)?

From Google' Gemini: Broadband Revolution (2000s-Present): Fiber to the home (FTTH) initiatives have brought ultra-fast internet speeds to consumers. Fiber offers significantly greater bandwidth and lower latency than traditional cable or DSL. FCC Broadband Map
